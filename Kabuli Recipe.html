Kabuli Pulao

Description: Atraditional Afghan rice dish with meat, carrots, raisins, almonds, and nuts.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Lamb or Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Raisins
  • Almonds
  • Spices like cumin and cardamom

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat with spices until tender.
  2. Satue grated carrots and raisins inghee.
  3. Boil the rice, then layer it with meat and sauteed mixture.
  4. Steam for a short time and serve
